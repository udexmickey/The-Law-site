Workers’ Compensation

Our Workers’ Compensation Law Group is actively engaged in commencing and defending workers’ compensation litigation. Our attorneys represent a broad range of employers and insurers across the state. We represent insurance agencies, employers, and third party administrators in all aspects of litigation. Additionally, we represent individual employees seeking assistance in navigating the workers’ compensation system after a workplace injury. Our lawyers’ extensive knowledge of the claims handling process and litigating before the South Dakota Department of Labor and Regulation is combined to provide the best value to our clients every step of the way.

Our lawyers are committed to providing clients with the highest level of legal representation through aggressive and responsive representation of our clients’ needs. We work hard to be available to all of our clients to meet their daily needs, including education and training for our clients with an eye towards helping employers and insurers avoid litigation. For those individuals who sustain a workplace injury, our attorneys pride themselves on being able to assist the individuals by taking from their extensive experience in workers’ compensation litigation and using that experience to obtain the best result for the client.

The experience, integrity, and ability to obtain the best results has earned our firm’s attorneys the respect of clients, colleagues, and opponents alike. Our attorneys handle a wide range of cases involving workers’ compensation and workplace injuries, from those involving minor injuries to those with permanent injuries.
