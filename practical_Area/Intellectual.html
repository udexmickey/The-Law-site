Foreclosures

Foreclosures require attention to detail and strict compliance with state law, federal protections, as well as particular terms and details contained in the contract between the parties. Our firm understands the law and the need to get it right. We represent lenders, creditors, landlords, tenants and individuals both in and outside of court to negotiate resolution of foreclosures of real property and personal property alike. Whether the issue involves a real estate mortgage, repossession, or lien foreclosures, our attorneys strive to provide affordable and timely resolution of these matters for our clients.
