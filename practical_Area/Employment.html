Employment Litigation

Law Firm has an active and varied employment litigation practice. Our clients include small businesses and large companies in a variety of industries, including manufacturing, banking, construction, non-profit services, insurance, and health care. Our employment litigation section also represents counties and municipalities with respect to their employment needs.

Our lawyers have significant experience handling discrimination claims as well as disputes under the Family Medical Leave Act, Equal Pay Act, Employment Retirement Income Security Act, and other statutes governing employee benefits. Our practice also includes handling other types of claims arising out of employment relationship, including unemployment, wrongful termination and breach of contract and non-compete/non-solicitation agreements.

Employment & Labor Law

Our attorneys have significant experience in providing employers with the support needed in their businesses, such as training, investigation, and policy and employment contract drafting. More specifically, our attorneys routinely provide workplace training to employees and supervisors. We are often retained to investigate workplace complaints such as harassment and discrimination. Our attorneys also are experienced in assisting employers with specific issues regarding employees such as reasonable accommodations, managing leave and absences, insurance benefit issues, and discipline/termination issues.

Please visit and subscribe to our Worker’s Compensation and Employment Blog at http://www.boycewceinsight.com/.

Absence Management

Our Workers’ Compensation Law Group, in connection with our Employment Litigation Group, offers absence management services to our clients in an effort to provide creative and practical solutions that help ensure that employers minimize employee absences and assist the employer in efficiently running their business. As part of this service, our attorneys offer advice on issues relating to employee leave, disability, and accommodations, as well as develop programs consistent with other goals of the employer. We pay close attention to providing the best value for our clients, and as part of that value, we respond to day-to-day questions from employers and insurers to ensure that the law is followed and provide some peace of mind to our clients.
